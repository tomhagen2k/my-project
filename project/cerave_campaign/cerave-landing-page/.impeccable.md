## Design Context

### Users
Gen Z skincare enthusiasts and busy professionals looking for clinical solutions. They value both effectiveness and a gentle, trustworthy experience. They are often "skintellectuals" who care about ingredients but want a premium, easy-to-digest interface.

### Brand Personality
**Trustworthy, Effective, Premium.**
The brand should feel clinical and science-backed (Trustworthy/Effective) but also elevated and sophisticated (Premium). It moves away from "drugstore" vibes towards a "modern laboratory" aesthetic.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Clean Clinical Light Mode.**
A bright, high-contrast, and airy design. Uses plenty of whitespace, refined typography, and a "Gentle Care" emotional tone. Avoids aggressive clinical visuals in favor of soft, natural, and refreshing imagery.

### Design Principles
1. **Clinical Sophistication**: Use precise typography and high-fidelity scientific illustrations to convey expertise.
2. **Premium Purity**: Lean into airy layouts and a "less is more" approach to spacing and content.
3. **Gentle Trust**: Incorporate soft, natural imagery and a calming color palette (OKLCH-based teals and warm neutrals).
4. **Interactive Clarity**: Use motion and progressive disclosure to explain complex science without overwhelming the user.
